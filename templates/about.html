About the Cosmic Carbon Calculator

Understand your footprint. Track it over time. Do something about it.

What this is

The Cosmic Carbon Calculator estimates the greenhouse gas emissions from everyday choices — how you get around, how you power and heat your home, and how many trees offset it all. Fill in a short assessment and get an instant breakdown, then come back over time to see how your footprint trends on your dashboard.

How it's calculated

Each category you enter is converted to kilograms of CO₂e using published per-unit emission factors, then combined into a single total. Trees on your property count as a negative offset against your total.

🚗 Transport
Car, bus, motorcycle, train, or bicycle — by weekly frequency
⚡ Energy
Gas, petroleum, coal, charcoal, electricity, or firewood
🏠 Housing
Scaled by number of homes on the account
🌳 Offset
Trees on your property reduce your net total

Your data

Every assessment you submit is saved to your account's history so your dashboard can chart trends over time. Only you can see your own entries.
